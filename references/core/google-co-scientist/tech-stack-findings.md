## Architecture synthesis

The public architecture is unusually clear at the logical level. Scientists provide a natural-language goal; a **Supervisor agent** converts that into a research plan, allocates compute, and dispatches work to specialized agents through a **task queue**. The system maintains **persistent context memory**, continuously gathers **summary statistics**, and iterates through generation, review, ranking, evolution, and meta-review. Agents call **external tools and APIs**, especially **web search**, and in some collaborations they can incorporate **specialized scientific models** such as AlphaFold.
