Want to learn more?

Here are some answers to common questions.

What makes NotebookLM different from other AI-powered note-taking apps?expand_more
As a research assistant, NotebookLM's advantage is being source-grounded. This helps deliver more accurate answers and insights based on your actual material — reducing the likelihood of AI errors and hallucinations.
How will the integration with Gemini AI improve NotebookLM?expand_more
Built with the latest Gemini model, NotebookLM gains a more nuanced understanding of your sources. This delivers more insightful summaries, helps identify deeper connections across documents, and provides more accurate answers to your questions.
What are the main advantages of NotebookLM compared to other AI learning apps?expand_more
NotebookLM offers several key advantages:
  • Direct control over sources: You provide the documents and information NotebookLM uses, ensuring the responses are grounded in your specific knowledge base.
  • Reduced hallucinations: By strictly referencing your uploaded sources, NotebookLM reduces the risk of the AI generating inaccurate information.
  • Personalized learning: NotebookLM creates a highly personalized learning and research environment tailored to your specific needs and content.
  • Efficiency in information synthesis: It dramatically speeds up the process of understanding, synthesizing, and extracting insights from large volumes of text.
  • Trust and reliability: The transparency of knowing exactly where the AI's information comes from builds greater trust in its output.
Can I use NotebookLM for creative projects like making a podcast?expand_more
Yes, NotebookLM can create a podcast-style Audio Overview of your materials. NotebookLM is a great tool for creative projects. It helps you organize research, summarize sources, and then brainstorm, draft, and refine your script with AI suggestions.
What are the most effective ways to integrate NotebookLM into a daily productivity workflow?expand_more
Some common ways you can use NotebookLM throughout your day include:
  • Starting your day with a document review — upload your meeting notes, reports and more to have NotebookLM analyze them and help you prepare for the day.
  • Use it as a research assistant — reduce your time “searching for the answer” and allow NotebookLM to clarify concepts, support drafting emails, prepare proposals and pull together relevant insights based on your sources.
  • Generate study or planning aids — prepare for meetings or exams with NotebookLM’s flashcards, quizzes, and dynamic reporting features.
  • End of day recap or planning — NotebookLM can summarize your notes from the day and help generate materials like to-do lists, preparing you for the day ahead.
Is my data privacy at risk with NotebookLM?expand_more
NotebookLM is built with robust security measures. Google does not directly use your content in NotebookLM for generative AI foundational model training unless you share feedback, see more details here.

If you are a Workspace user, your uploads, queries and the model's responses are not used to train generative AI models, even if you submit feedback.
Who has access to the notes and data I store in NotebookLM?expand_more
NotebookLM does not share your data with third parties. Learn how NotebookLM protects your data.
What is Google's policy on data retention for my deleted notebooks in NotebookLM?expand_more
There is currently no recovery option for deleted notes.
Privacy Policy
How do I report a result in NotebookLM that I believe creates a safety concern or is inappropriate?expand_more
Your feedback is essential, especially regarding safety. If you encounter a NotebookLM response that is inaccurate, or that you feel is unsafe or inappropriate, please use one of these methods to report it:

  • Report the specific response: Click the thumbs-down icon next to the answer and select the category that matches your concern.
  • Send general feedback: Navigate to the settings menu and use the "Send feedback" option to provide more details.
For step-by-step instructions on reporting, please consult the NotebookLM Help Center.
